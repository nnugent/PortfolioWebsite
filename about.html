About Me

While I was growing up my Father was in the U.S. Navy, and as a result we moved frequently. I have lived over seas a number of times and in two different countries. I was born in Jacksonville Florida. We then moved to Lombard, Illinois, and then to Roda, Spain. When we got to Spain my Dad's entire squadron got moved to Whidbey Island, in Washington State. After two years there we moved to Honolulu, Hawai'i where my Dad retired and then we had to move to Mililani, Hawai'i because we could no longer live on base. After a few more years there we got tired of the ridiculous Hawai'i prices and moved to Eagle, Wisconsin, which is where I live now.

Activity

Throughout my elementary and middle school years I was extremely active, I skateboarded many hours each day and played team sports, including mainly baseball and soccer.

Technology

As I got older my interest in technology grew, and I began to take computer and networking classes in high school Eventually I realized that this is what I was passionate about.
